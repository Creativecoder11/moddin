## Homepage layout and copy

### 1) Hero
**Headline:** Bangladesh, Made Easier to Enter
**Subheadline:** Moddin helps global investors, companies, and institutions understand the market, access the right stakeholders, and move from interest to execution.
**Ticker Items:** Bangladesh Market Entry • Investment Facilitation • Institutional Access • Strategic Matchmaking • Execution Support

### 2) Opportunity snapshot (`#why`)
**Section title:** Why Bangladesh, Why Now.
**Intro line:** Bangladesh is no longer defined by manufacturing alone. It is becoming a strategic destination for global businesses seeking scale, efficiency, and long-term growth.
**Stats:**
* 176m Population - The 8th largest consumer base in the world.
* $460bn GDP - Consistent mid-single-digit real growth over the past decade.
* $55bn+ Exports - Led by textiles, leather, jute, and a fast-rising digital economy.
* 8th Workforce - Largest workforce globally, with deep industrial and technical depth.

### 3) The gap
**Intro:** Interest in Bangladesh is strong. Conversion into real investment and partnerships is where friction begins.
* i. What Bangladesh already has: Scale, export strength, industrial depth, and cost advantage. (A proven manufacturing base, a young and ambitious workforce, and two decades of consistent export-led growth.)
* ii. What investors still face: Fragmented access, regulatory complexity, and difficulty finding the right local pathways. (Navigating ministries, chambers, and capital partners without trusted intermediaries slows every decision.)
* iii. What this leads to: Delayed decisions, missed opportunities, and weaker market entry outcomes. (Strong interest stalls short of execution — not because the thesis is wrong, but because the path isn't clear.)

### 4) Moddin’s role
* 01 Position (Opportunity Definition): We shape how Bangladesh is understood—through clear market narratives, sector insights, and investor-ready positioning.
* 02 Connect (Strategic Access): We connect you to institutions, investors, and partners that matter—turning access into aligned opportunity.
* 03 Execute (Execution & Delivery): We support market entry, partnerships, and transactions—ensuring progress from first step to measurable result.

### 5) Services (`#services`)
**Section title:** What We Do.
**Intro line:** Four capabilities across the market-entry journey—from first insight to signed partnership and operating presence in Bangladesh.

* 01 Trade & Market Expansion (For global companies): Enable cross-border trade through structured market access, sourcing alignment, and partner connections across Bangladesh’s industrial ecosystem.
* 02 Investment & Deal Facilitation (For investors & corporates): Support investors from opportunity discovery to deal execution—covering partnerships, structuring, and market entry.
* 03 Country Branding & Positioning (For governments & national platforms): Shape how Bangladesh is understood globally—through clear investment narratives, sector positioning, and investor-facing communication.
* 04 Policy & Institutional Access (For institutions & partners): Navigate regulatory pathways and unlock access to ministries, agencies, and key institutions—enabling smoother entry and execution.

### 6) Who we work with (`#who`)
**Section title:** Who We Work With.
**Intro line:** Different partners, different objectives—supported by the same structured approach and local capability.

* Global Companies (For companies): Exploring sourcing, expansion, regional strategy, and operational setup in Bangladesh.
* Investors & Funds (For capital): Capital looking at high-growth opportunities, partnerships, and long-term market access.
* Government & Institutions (For institutions): Agencies, embassies, chambers, and development organizations supporting trade and investment flows.
* Bangladeshi Companies (For BD firms): Local firms seeking capital, global positioning, strategic partnerships, and cross-border growth.

### 7) Process section
* i. Explore: Understand the market, the objective, and the opportunity.
* ii. Structure: Define the right entry path, stakeholder map, and partnership model.
* iii. Engage: Activate introductions, coordination, and opportunity development.
* iv. Execute: Move into setup, deal progress, and on-ground support.

### 8) Insights / resources (`#insights`)
* Bangladesh Opportunity Briefs: Short, structured primers on where the market is going — and what that means for capital and partners.
* Sector Notes & Market Signals: On-the-ground reads across textiles, digital, energy, logistics, agri, and financial services.
* Market Entry Guides & Checklists: Step-by-step playbooks covering setup, licensing, partner selection, and first 180 days.
